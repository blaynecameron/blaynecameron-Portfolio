My name is Blayne Cameron Chong and I am a User Experience Designer. I solve problems by designing and creating digital products and services that enrich people's lives.

I compare my design process to putting together a puzzle. Looking at a single piece of a puzzle it is difficult to see the big picture. But, it is my role to take these arbitrary pieces and place them together into a coherent picture that people can understand and appreciate.
